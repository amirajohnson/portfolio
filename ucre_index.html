UCRE Word Shuffle!

Created by Amira Johnson using HTML, CSS, and Javascript!
Welcome to my UCRE Word Shuffle game! Embedded in the hints of this game are the things I've learned from the course 05-410, User Centered Research and Evaluation. I designed an interactive word scramble game to reflect the newfound skills in usability testing, interviews, and user research that I learned during my course on UX design and GenAI systems. Each level represents something I learned from a module, with hints tied to my learning goals. Click the button below to see our final project, ImpactLens' website!
